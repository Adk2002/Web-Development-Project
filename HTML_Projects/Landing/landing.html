Star Fish

The Starfish, also known as sea stars, are fascinating marine animals belonging to the phylum Echinodermata. Starfish typically have five arms, although some species may have more.

Clown Fish

The Clownfish, known for their vibrant colors and distinctive patterns. These fish are widely recognized for their association with sea anemones species.

Madarin Fish

The Blue Mandarin Fish, scientifically known as Synchiropus splendidus, is a small and brilliantly colored saltwater fish native to the Pacific.
